## QuantConnect

QuantConnect is an algorithmic trading platform that supports research, backtesting, and live deployment of quantitative strategies across asset classes. Its connection with dYdX enables live trading of Crypto Futures on the dYdX decentralized exchange through the QuantConnect interface.

The partnership leverages QuantConnect's infrastructure to manage communications with the dYdX API, letting users concentrate on strategy development and algorithm oversight. QuantConnect's LEAN engine provides AI models, technical indicators, and datasets.

For details, see the [integration documentation](https://qnt.co/dydx-docs).
